Our Story

Grown close to home, arranged with care

We opened our doors in 2012, in a narrow shop on Byres Road with good light and a leaky back door. Iona had spent years on the wholesale flower markets and wanted to do things differently - seasonal, a little wild, never the same bunch twice.

We work with Scottish growers wherever the season allows - ranunculus and sweet peas in spring, dahlias and cosmos through the late summer - topping up with the best of the Dutch market for the rest. Nothing is forced into a foam-block. Everything is hand-tied so it keeps growing the way it wants to.

Pop your head in, tell us who the flowers are for, and we'll make something just for them. That's always been the whole idea.
